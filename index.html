Uber Drops Out Of Ride-Hailing Race

Ride-hailing company Uber intends on shutting down its money-making ride-hailing operation, a spokesperson with the company told The Plug over the phone.

Tesla Stops Marketing Its Self-Driving Car Tech As 'Autopilot'

Following massive amounts of criticism from the electic car community and government officials, Tesla CEO Elon Musk announced his company would no longer be calling its self-driving driver asistance feature as "Autopilot." This news comes after two fatalities in Tesla vehicles that were allegedly being driven with Autopilot engaged.
